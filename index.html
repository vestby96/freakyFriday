Calculator

This calculator can be used to find the answer to simple mathematical equations.
It can calculate plus, minus, division and multiplication.
It can also handle decimals and negative numbers.

To use the calculator you can either click on to input-box, or your can click on the buttons that represent numbers and sign.
The answer will appear in the input-box where the equation was, you can continue to do calculations with the answer you got. Use the clear button to remove everything from the input-box

Example: 3+5=
Solution 1: first click on the "3" button, then click on "5", then click in "=" and the calculator will give you the answer
Solution 2: click on the input-box at the topand type in the equation, then click on the "=" button
